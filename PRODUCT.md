# Aura — AI Girlfriend Chat App

## Users
Young men (18–30) who want a fun, entertaining AI companion to talk to anytime. They want witty banter, playful flirting, and a personality that feels real and engaging.

## Brand
- Name: Aura
- Tone: Warm, flirty, playful, confident
- Feel: Premium, modern, slightly mysterious

## Core Features (MVP)
- AI girlfriend chat with a distinct, engaging personality
- Flirty, witty, contextual conversation
- Simple, clean chat interface

## Anti-references
- No robotic, stiff, or clinical tone
- No overly complex UI — keep it clean and focused
