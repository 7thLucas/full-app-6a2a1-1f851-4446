# Design Guidelines — Aura

## Colors
- Background: Deep dark (#0D0D1A) 
- Primary accent: Soft purple (#9B59B6) to pink (#E91E8C) gradient
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) and light gray (#CCCCCC)
- Card/surface: Dark purple-tinted (#1A1A2E)

## Typography
- Headings: Bold, modern sans-serif
- Body: Clean, readable sans-serif
- Tone: Elegant but approachable

## Components
- Chat bubbles: Rounded, gradient accent for AI responses
- Input: Clean dark input with subtle glow on focus
- Navigation: Minimal, bottom tab or sidebar

## Feel
Premium dark-mode aesthetic, like a high-end mobile app. Subtle glows and gradients. Nothing cluttered.
